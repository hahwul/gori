**Sit in the loop.**

gori (고리 — Korean for *ring, link, loop*) is a keyboard-driven HTTP/HTTPS intercepting proxy and web-hacking workbench that runs entirely in your terminal. It sits in the loop between your client and the target, turns every request into a pentest surface, and lets you capture, intercept, repeater, fuzz, and scan — without leaving the shell.
