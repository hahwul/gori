One tool, the whole assessment.

History, Intercept, Repeater, Fuzzer, the Probe scanner, Param Miner, and Issues live in a single project, every flow one keystroke away.

Keyboard-native A palette-driven TUI built with Crystal. No mouse required, no browser tab to babysit.

Scriptable and AI Everything reachable headless via gori run, and open to AI agents through the built-in MCP server.

From capture to issue.

Capture

Run the proxy, trust the CA once, and every request flows into History with full HTTP/2, WebSocket, and gRPC detail.

Test

Send interesting flows to Repeater or the Fuzzer, mine hidden parameters, and let Probe flag issues as you browse.

Report

Triage results into Issues, take notes, and export, or hand the whole project to an agent over MCP.

Or hand it to an agent.

gori has no chat window baked in. It exposes your project over MCP, so any agent (Claude, Codex, Grok) reads your captured traffic and drives the tools through one clean seam. You pick the model; your traffic stays where you put it.
